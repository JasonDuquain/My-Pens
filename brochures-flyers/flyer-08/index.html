Pasta is Italy's gift to the world

and is now naturalised in dozens of countries as a satisfying dish that suits the needs of today's busy cooks and appeals to both adults and children. It can be a simple dough of flour and water or enriched with eggs, made into hundreds of different shapes, left plain or coloured and favoured with such ingredients as spinach, beetroot, tomato, chili or squid ink, and is available fresh or dried.
